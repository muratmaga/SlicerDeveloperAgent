===============================================
Adding New AI Models - Quick Reference
===============================================

GitHub regularly adds new models to their API.
Update your DeveloperAgent in 3 simple steps:

STEP 1: Check Available Models
-------------------------------
Visit: https://github.com/marketplace/models
Look for new models and note their model IDs

STEP 2: Edit Resources/prompts_config.py
-----------------------------------------
Open Resources/prompts_config.py and find AVAILABLE_MODELS:

    AVAILABLE_MODELS = [
        ("Display Name", "model-id"),
        ...
        # Add your new model:
        ("New Model Name", "new-model-id"),
    ]

Optional - change default:
    DEFAULT_MODEL = "gpt-4o"  # or your preferred model

STEP 3: Reload Module in Slicer
--------------------------------
In Slicer Python console:
    >>> import importlib
    >>> import DeveloperAgent
    >>> importlib.reload(DeveloperAgent)

The new models will appear in the dropdown!
